Key Research Projects

University of Ottawa / OHRI

MR-DTI of the Spinal Cord

Diffusion Tensor Imaging studies of spinal cord with acute and chronic injury — advancing MRI correlation with motor/sensory deficits.

Carleton University Collaboration

Thrombin Aptamer Relaxivity

Proof-of-concept relaxivity study showing target binding improves relaxivity in aptamer–gadolinium conjugates.

University of Ottawa

PLGA Hollow Fibre Channel

MRI-based in vitro degradation study of nanoparticle-loaded PLGA hollow fibre channels for spinal cord injury repair.

Ganga Hospital, Coimbatore

Cervical Spondylosis DTI

DTI study characterising neurological states in cervical spondylosis myelopathy — published in Journal of MR Imaging.

Ganga Hospital, Coimbatore

Lumbar Disc Mechanics

ISSLS Prize–winning study of effects of in vivo mechanical forces on human lumbar discs using scoliotic disc as biological model.

CARE, Chennai

Alzheimer's Disease Imaging

Quantitative assessment of hippocampal/corpus callosum volumes and functional connectivity changes in Alzheimer's disease staging.
